### Competitive Positioning
1636 Forum has an effectively **uncontested niche**:
1. Only independent Harvard alumni newsletter with editorial independence
2. "Apolitical and narrowly focused" differentiates from partisan outlets
3. 30K+ subscribers provides meaningful scale in a very specific audience
4. Covers governance, free speech, antisemitism issues that official channels avoid
5. Every.org presence signals nonprofit/community legitimacy

### Key Vulnerability
- Over 50% of ad-acquired subscribers have no Harvard affiliation
- The niche is extremely narrow — broad Meta targeting inevitably captures non-alumni
- CTA language and hooks must explicitly reference "Harvard alumni" to self-select
- Lookalike audiences built from actual alumni lists are the highest-quality targeting option
