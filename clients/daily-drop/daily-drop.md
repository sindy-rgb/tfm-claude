## 3. BRAND VOICE RULES

### NEVER Rules
1. **NEVER say "travel hack(s)"** — explicitly banned
2. **NEVER say "free lounge access"** — explicitly banned
3. **NEVER show a physical credit card** at any point
4. **NEVER show a credit card inside a digital phone wallet**
5. **NEVER mention specific credit card brand names**
6. **NEVER use jargon or advanced points terminology** — audience is beginners

### Approved Tone
- Friendly, clear, approachable, educational, non-technical
- "Daily Drop helps people understand points easily — you don't need to be an expert"
- Curiosity-driven: "Most people don't realize..."
- "5 minutes a day" micro-commitment framing
