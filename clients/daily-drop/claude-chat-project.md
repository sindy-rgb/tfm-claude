**Key Creative Principles for Daily Drop:**
- **Simplicity wins.** The audience doesn't know what a transfer partner is. Don't explain — inspire.
- **Aspiration over education.** Show the trip, not the process. "Imagine waking up in Bali" > "Here's how to transfer 50K points to Hyatt."
- **Credit score as entry point.** TFM 130 proved that "high credit score = free luxury travel" is the strongest ROI hook. Lean into credit score as a qualification filter.
- **Micro-commitment framing.** "5 minutes a day" makes the newsletter feel effortless. Low barrier = high conversion.
- **IG-first.** Instagram leads monetize 2x+ better than Facebook leads. Design creative that stops the scroll on Instagram, then adapt for Facebook — not the other way around.
- **Partnership wrapper for older demographics.** If targeting retirees or older travelers, ALWAYS use a creator/partnership angle (like Retirement Traveler). Solo retiree ads have poor ROI.
