## 3. BRAND VOICE RULES

### NEVER Rules
- **NEVER** use hype, hustle culture, or aggressive sales language — Jay's world is peace, not hustle
- **NEVER** make ads feel like a performance — they must feel like a personal moment
- **NEVER** use Gary Vee-style energy — Jay's tone is mindfulness and calm reflection, not yelling
- **NEVER** position Jay as only one thing (just meditation, just relationships, just purpose) — his work spans the full spectrum of mindful living
- **NEVER** use formal brand guideline assets that haven't been approved — no brand guideline (colors/type/logo pack) was provided yet; follow Working Insights doc for tone and use existing Jay Shetty visual cues

### Approved Voice & Language
- **Soft authority, not hype** — "Jay's voice is soft authority, not hype. Ads must feel like a personal moment, not a performance."
- **Emotive / lifestyle visual identity** — more emotive than minimalist (contrast with James Clear's minimalism)
- **Core positioning hooks:** "Former monk," "#1 mental health podcast," "helped millions improve their relationships"
- **Approved angles:** Authority/credibility intro ("Hi, I'm Jay Shetty..."), clarity over hustle ("You don't need to grind 24/7 to grow — clarity is the new hustle"), emotional resonance + habit authority fusion ("One mindful habit that rewires your day")
- **Newsletter name:** The Daily Wisdom
- **Tagline:** "Live better every day" (Updated March 2026)
- **Tone:** Reflective, warm, personal, wise — as if Jay is speaking directly to one person
- **Voice cloning:** ElevenLabs clone of Jay's voice exists and is approved for use in voiceover creatives (see DCT_121 brief)

### Newsletter Voice Patterns (from automated newsletter analysis, March 2026) (Updated March 2026)
- **Structure every issue follows:** Hook question/bold statement -> Personal story or reader anecdote -> Core insight with bolded takeaways -> Framework or numbered list -> "Today's Wiser Choice" (one practical action) -> "On Purpose" podcast feature -> Sponsor section -> Warm sign-off ("With love, Jay")
- **Recurring expressions:** "The Daily Wisdom," "Today's Wiser Choice," "On Purpose," "live better every day," "protecting your energy/peace," "alignment," "create vs. react," "genuine expression," "inner state"
- **Voice characteristics:** First-person and confessional ("I've learned," "I've been there too"), empathetic before prescriptive, gentle but motivating, science meets soul (references Kahneman, Dr. Matthew Walker), question-led hooks that replay relatable internal scenarios (e.g., "Is your brain constantly replaying conversations at 2AM?")
- **Key themes (March 2026):** Inner alignment over external achievement, energy management and boundaries, communication as practice, intentional mornings, authentic self-expression
