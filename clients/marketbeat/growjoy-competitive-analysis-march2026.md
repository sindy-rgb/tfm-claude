## SECTION 6: ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS — WHY THE GAP EXISTS

### The gap is NOT creative quality or CPL. The gap is LP/SMS strategy.

```
GrowJoy Rev/User: $5.42
TFM Rev/User:     $4.82
Delta:            $0.60/user

GrowJoy revenue leads per Meta lead: 1,928 SMS + 7,720 rev leads / 5,699 website leads = 1.35x
TFM revenue leads per Meta lead:     284 SMS + 1,655 rev leads / 1,558 website leads = 1.06x
```

**GrowJoy generates 35% more revenue-attributable leads per Meta pixel event than TFM.** This is because:

1. **SMS opt-in checkbox on LP** → Every SMS opt-in becomes a revenue lead (gets blasts with affiliate offers)
2. **PDF report offer framing** → Higher perceived value = more form completions = more co-registration revenue
3. **"$29.97 → FREE" anchoring** → Creates reciprocity, users more willing to give SMS number

**If TFM matched GrowJoy's SMS capture rate (1.35x vs 1.06x), our ROAS would jump from 41.8% to approximately 53.3% — EXCEEDING GrowJoy.**
