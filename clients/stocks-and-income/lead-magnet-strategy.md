## 1. Lead Magnet Ideas

Specific lead magnets to create around ad angles:

### Top 25 Stocks (Twist)
- Back-to-front twist on the classic "top 10 stocks" format

### SpaceX Derivatives
- Stocks that will benefit from SpaceX IPO

### IPO Pipeline
- Top companies likely to IPO this year and derivatives (Stripe, etc.)

### Energy/Nuclear → AI
- AI buildout is consuming massive electricity
- OpenAI/Anthropic battle to go public first — derivatives of this
  - They raise hundreds of billions in this scenario
- Standalone angle: M7 valuations will go up dramatically during IPO

### Congress Insider Trading (Autopilot, Quiverquant)
- **Newscast ad (red)** — talk about politicians making $X trading. Americans tired of politicians trading when others can't. [Reference ad](https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/?id=2062391881009079)
- **Podcast conversation** — "so there's a newsletter that tells you what stocks politicians are trading"
- **Anti-Autopilot** — apps charge $20+ per politician you follow; we give away the 10 biggest trades Politicians just made for FREE
- **How to trade like a member of congress**
- **How f*cked is it** the politicians you voted for are using your tax money to insider trade → We give away their trades for free

### Social Arbitrage
- **Chris Camillo angle** — averaged XX% YoY return for 17 years doing social arbitrage
  - Spends all day reading TikTok comments and Reddit
  - "We did it for you" — Top 10 social arbitrage stocks set to explode in 2026
- **Hedge fund data angle** — How do massive hedge funds get an edge?
  - They spend MILLIONS on data (Tikker-mapped social media sentiment, scraping Reddit)
  - Average dataset is $1M/yr (EagleAlpha, Neudata)
  - Hundreds of MIT and Harvard statisticians
  - "Our team has used that same data to create a list of the top 10 stocks set to explode in 2026 and we're giving it away for FREE"
- **Reddit angle** — "Redditors aren't smarter than you, they're just chronically online 40 year olds in their basement"
- **2020 power shift** — Robinhood $20B → $200B, retail can move markets, attention economy shifted to Reddit & Twitter
