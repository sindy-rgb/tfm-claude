1. **Concept Database** (`collection://263f4a12-6e06-800c-8885-000bd02eedb4`)

## 3. WEBSITE & FUNNEL ANALYSIS

### experientialhospitality.com — Full Analysis

#### Value Proposition & Positioning
- **Core positioning:** "The expert guidance you need to create and operate world-class, one-of-a-kind destinations"
- **Anti-Airbnb angle:** Deliberately positions against generic Airbnb/STR investing. EH = branded experiential properties, not automation.
- **Proof point:** Live Oak Lake — built for $2.3M, generated $1M bookings in year one, 80%+ direct booking rate, sold for $7M in 2023 ($1M/key exit valuation)
- **Target audience:** Landowners, existing STR investors seeking differentiation, entrepreneurs wanting branded hospitality properties

#### Product Details
- **Masterclass:** 45 video episodes, 8-10 hours, 10 modules
- **Modules:** Introduction, Market & Location, Finance, Vision & Design, Design & Build, Preparing to Launch, Marketing, Operations & Automation, Team Management, Case Studies
- **Coaching:** 5 weeks of live group coaching calls (synchronized with weekly module releases)
- **Expert panel:** 11 contributors including Hans Lorei (design), Mitchell Baldridge (tax CPA), Andrew Kitchell (revenue management), Joseph Tindell (smart home tech), Nate Vietz (social media)
- **Included assets:** Custom financial underwriting model, real estate fundamentals guide, guest messaging deck, welcome book template, 1 year free community access
- **Community renewal:** $247/month or $1,997/year after first year
- **Pricing:** Not publicly displayed — requires sales call

#### Primary Funnel: Meta Ads → Webinar → Sales Call
1. **Meta Ads** — DCT creatives drive to webinar registration page
2. **Webinar Registration** — WebinarKit (formerly WebinarJam) registration flow at `eh.experientialhospitality.com` (requires form fill: name, email, phone)
3. **Live Webinar** — 90-minute live training, hosted by Isaac, every ~2 weeks
4. **Replay** — Sent to registrants who miss live; replay view rates have been strong (12.2% in Feb vs 6.6% Jan avg)
5. **Sales Call Booking** — CTA during/after webinar to book via GHL widget
6. **Sales Call** — Closes into masterclass enrollment
7. **Community Upsell** — Circle community with bi-weekly expert calls

## 4. COMPETITOR RESEARCH

### A. Direct Competitors: Micro-Resort / Glamping Education

#### 1. American Glamping Association (AGA)
- **URL:** americanglampingassociation.net
- **Offering:** 100% online professional development courses; strategic learning report; business pitch deck
- **Pricing:** Not publicly listed (membership required)
- **Positioning:** Industry association / credentialing body — broad glamping focus
- **Audience:** Glamping site operators, aspiring owners
- **Funnel:** Membership → Courses → Conference (Glamping Show Americas, Sep 29-30, 2026, Denver CO)
- **Differentiation from EH:** AGA is an industry association, not a founder-led masterclass. Less personal, more institutional. No founder proof story.

#### 2. Glampitect Academy
- **URL:** connect.glampitect.com/glampitect-academy-course-2024
- **Offering:** Free 4-week, 2-hour quarterly course on planning, building, and launching glamping businesses
- **Pricing:** Free (lead gen for consulting services)
- **Positioning:** UK-based glamping consultancy turned educator
- **Funnel:** Free Academy → Consulting services upsell
- **Differentiation from EH:** Free introductory course (lower commitment); consulting-first model; UK/EU focus; no high-ticket community play

#### 3. Heather Blankenship Glamping Course
- **URL:** courses.heatherblankenship.com/glampingcourse
- **Offering:** 2+ hour course, 25+ modules on launching and scaling glamping resorts
- **Positioning:** Blueprint for "passive income" through glamping
- **Differentiation from EH:** Smaller scope, positioned as passive income play (which EH deliberately avoids). No founder exit story or expert panel.

#### 4. Stout Tent — Glamping Business Blueprint
- **URL:** stouttent.com/the-glamping-business-blueprint-online-course/
- **Offering:** Online course with templates, marketing resources, step-by-step guidance, lifetime access
- **Positioning:** "Complete blueprint" for glamping business
- **Differentiation from EH:** Tent manufacturer offering education as lead gen for equipment sales. Different business model entirely.

#### 5. Inspired Courses / Sarah Riley — Glamping Business Academy
- **URL:** inspiredcoursesvip.com/glamping-business-academy
- **Offering:** Academy program + "Ultimate Glamping Business Start-Up Guide"
- **Positioning:** Business plan development, ROI understanding, unique path to success
- **Differentiation from EH:** More generalist glamping focus; no micro-resort specialization or $7M exit proof point
