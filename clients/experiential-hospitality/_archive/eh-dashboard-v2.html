Key Performance Insights
DCT 107 is the all-time champion — 14 deals, $80,469 revenue across 7 months. Every UGC webinar iteration of this creative closes deals.
December was the peak month — $91,713 from 14 deals. Scaling spend to $16K proved higher spend = proportionally more closes without CPL degradation.
CPL recovering after Feb spike — From $7.89 peak back down to $5.95. Pacing strategy and creative refresh working.
7-Day ECC has the highest ROAS (1.94x) — The email course pre-qualifies buyers better than webinar alone. Only 8 deals though, so scale opportunity is massive.
Replay rate improving — Jumped to 12.2% in Feb cycle. Secondary conversion path gaining strength as more registrants watch replays.
Call show rate (78.9% via GHL) exceeds the cycle-level 63.6% — Isaac's follow-up sequences are elite and closing the gap between booked and showed.
Registration to call booking (3.6%) is the single biggest funnel leak. Fixing this from 3.6% to 5% = $180K additional revenue at current rates.
6 deals (~$35K) have broken/missing UTM attribution. Two show raw {{adset.name}} macro. Fix the template in Ads Manager to improve tracking.
February and March ROAS declining — 0.73x in Feb, 0.14x in March (early/lagging). Attribution lag typical for high-ticket (30-60 day close cycle).
Strategic Recommendations
SCALE: Increase to $20-25K per webinar cycle. December proved higher spend = more closes. The current $15K cap leaves revenue on the table. The funnel economics support 2.5x+ return at scale.
DOUBLE DOWN on DCT 107: Create 3-4 new UGC iterations with fresh hooks. This creative family has proven ROAS across 7 months and 14 deals. Protect it with constant iteration.
FIX THE CALL BOOK LEAK: The 3.6% call book rate is the #1 bottleneck. Test: stronger webinar CTA, post-webinar email optimization, SMS nudges, and retargeting registrants who didn't book within 48h.
INVEST IN ECC FUNNEL: Highest ROAS (1.94x) but only 8 deals. Dedicate 20% of budget to scaling ECC ads for higher-quality pipeline.
FIX UTMs IMMEDIATELY: Two active deals show raw {{adset.name}}. Update the UTM template in Ads Manager to close the attribution gap before the next cycle.
WORK THE 1,376 LEAD BACKLOG: GHL shows 1,376 leads sitting in "New Leads" stage untouched. Even a 2% close rate on those = 27 deals = ~$162K in revenue.
