## 3. BRAND VOICE RULES

### NEVER Rules (Compliance-Critical)
1. **NEVER imply stock recommendations or picks** — SEC compliance issue
2. **NEVER claim risk ratio evaluations** — App does not make risk/reward assessments
3. **NEVER call the scanner "AI Scanner"** — It presents real-time data, not AI analysis
4. **NEVER claim alerts are SMS if they require the app**
5. **NEVER say "free" unless true for all features**
6. **NEVER misrepresent politician trade tracking** — Trades reported within 30 days
7. **Use "might" instead of definitive claims**

### Approved Language
- "Stocks Dot News" (spoken) or "Stocks.News" (written)
- Feature-specific descriptions: "Breaking news alerts delivered through the app"
- Competitive framing: Scanner competitors charge $100–$160/mo; Stocks.News is $9.99/mo
