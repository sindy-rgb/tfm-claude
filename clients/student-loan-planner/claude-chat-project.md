## Brand Voice Rules

**NEVER Rules:**
- NEVER overpromise guaranteed loan forgiveness — this is a regulated financial space
- NEVER use financial fearmongering or overtly negative tone — creates clarity during chaos, not fear
- NEVER reference political opinions or partisan positions on student loan policy
- NEVER use generic "debt help" language — ALWAYS profession-specific ("While other dentists...", not "While other borrowers...")
- NEVER position SLP as a lender or servicer — they are an educational and consulting platform
- NEVER conflate SLP with government programs — SLP is an independent advisor

**Approved Voice:** Conversational, profession-specific. Pain > Relief > Empowerment arc: "You're tired of guessing" > "Here's how to make it manageable" > "You deserve clarity." Position SLP as the trusted expert simplifying chaos. Repeat themes: confusion, anxiety, overpayment, and hope through clarity.

**Approved Copy Patterns:**
- "Student Loan Planner is the free weekly newsletter trusted by professionals with six-figure student loan debt."
- "Read Student Loan Planner's free newsletter, made for high-debt, high-income professionals like you."
- "This free newsletter explains student loan strategies in 5 minutes."
- "Luckily, this free newsletter shows you exactly what to do next." (strongest payoff line)

**Winning Formats:**
1. Text-over-B-roll video — dominant format across all top performers
2. ElevenLabs voiceover
3. Static images (memes, "dear email" format, fake tweets)
4. UGC testimonial (raw, unpolished)
