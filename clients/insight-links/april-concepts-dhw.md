## DCT_142 — "The Decision-Maker's Blind Spot"
**Framework:** Text Callout Static (pain-point amplification) + Curiosity Gap
**Audience track:** Dual — clinician executives AND non-clinician leaders
**Format:** Static (text-heavy) + TOV variant
**Rationale:** DCT_122 "Make Better Decisions" delivered the highest DHW qualification rate (28.2%) at $11.18 qualified CPL with only $123 spend. The "decisions" framing pulls VP/C-suite profiles. This concept takes that signal and amplifies it with a specific text-callout pain point: digital health leaders are making decisions with incomplete market context because they lack time to track the full landscape. Pairs the proven text-callout format (DCT_118's success on CW) with DHW's decision-maker audience.

**Creative 2 — Text-heavy static (white text on dark)**

The executives who track this in real time make better decisions than the ones who catch up quarterly. Digital Health Wire: 5 minutes, twice a week, 35,000+ leaders."
