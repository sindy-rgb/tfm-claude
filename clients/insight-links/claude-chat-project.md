## Brand Voice Rules

**NEVER Rules:**
- NEVER use casual or consumer-grade language — audience is MDs, PhDs, executives
- NEVER make clinical claims or promise patient outcomes
- NEVER use clickbait — clinicians are "allergic to inbox clutter"
- NEVER use AI-generated sounding copy — Jake wants natural human voice
- NEVER target or speak to patients, retired professionals, or students
- NEVER report raw CPL without qualified CPL — Jake does not trust raw CPL alone
- NEVER let Imaging Wire stagnate without visible action — IW = 65% of revenue

**Approved Voice:** Professional but accessible. "Easy and accessible" vs. dense medical journals. Time-saving framing: "stay current," "filter the noise," "caught up fast." Authority positioning: "curated," "context for smarter decisions," "what actually matters." Confession-style and peer-to-peer hooks work best. Text-heavy ads outperform visual creative.

**Winning Formats:** Confession-style TOV (dominant), Life Hack TOV, Apple Notes Transformation, Replace Journals
