> **The premise of this report:** Raw CPL is noise. Qualified CPL is signal. But "qualified" itself has two dimensions: form engagement (QUALIFIED field) and audience fit (ICP title match). This report shows BOTH side by side, because they tell different stories.

---

## How the Dual Metrics Work

| Metric | What It Measures | Strength | Weakness |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Form Qualified** | Subscriber completed the ICP qualification form | Measures engagement/intent | Passes non-ICP titles; misses busy professionals who skip forms |
| **ICP Title Match** | Subscriber's TITLE field matches newsletter-specific ICP keywords | Measures actual audience fit | Title field may be empty or inaccurate; does not measure engagement |
| **Both (Qual + ICP)** | Passes BOTH filters | Highest-confidence quality signal | Smallest count -- conservative |

**Four quadrants per DCT:**
- **REAL WINNER** = High Form Qual + High ICP (right people who engage)
- **THE TRAP** = High Form Qual + Low ICP (form-completers not the target audience)
- **HIDDEN QUALITY** = Low Form Qual + High ICP (right people who skip the form)
- **KILL CANDIDATE** = Low Form Qual + Low ICP

## Recommendations for April (Updated)

### 1. Adopt "Both" as the primary quality metric for client reporting
Form Qualified alone over-counts on CW (passes non-cardiac titles) and under-counts on DHW (misses busy executives). Both = Form Qual AND ICP Title Match is the most honest metric. Lead with Both CPL in Jake conversations.

### 2. Build ICP-filtering creative for CW
CW's core problem: broad creative attracts broad audiences. The "If you, BUT" format that works on DHW needs a CW variant: "If you work in cardiology BUT can't keep up with every ACC update..." This bakes ICP filtering into the creative itself.

### 3. Investigate DHW form completion rates
DHW has 83 more ICP matches than form-qualifiers. These are real executives skipping the form. Options:
- Simplify the form (fewer fields)
- Make form completion incentivized (exclusive content gate)
- Accept that busy executives will never complete forms and use title-matching as the DHW quality metric

### 4. Weekly ICP title match reporting
Add ICP title match counts to the Friday report alongside form-qualified counts. Both metrics together prevent blind spots.
