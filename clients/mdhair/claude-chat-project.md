## Brand Voice Rules

**NEVER Rules:**
- NEVER position Minoxidil as negative — MDhair offers Minoxidil to some customers. CAN position as better than Finasteride (side effects) and hair transplants (invasive/expensive). Pooja explicitly killed anti-Minoxidil messaging.
- NEVER misspell the brand name — it is "MDhair" (no space, capital M and D). Not "MD Hair", "MdHair", or "MDHair."
- NEVER use a CTA other than "Take the quiz" — do not rewrite to alternatives
- NEVER omit the "up to 60% off" offer in relevant creative
- NEVER skip the AI-generated content disclaimer — all AI-generated content must be labeled with "AI-generated" disclosure
- NEVER reference money-back guarantees without T&Cs indicator
- NEVER use generic branded statics — client produces 10-15 internally per month and they haven't scaled. Don't recreate formats they've already exhausted.
- NEVER use fake or unverifiable claims — no fake tweets, fabricated "featured in" claims, nothing that can be researched and proven untrue

**Approved Voice:** Scientific but accessible. Emotional validation — especially for shedding, postpartum, confidence recovery. Key stats: 88.9% reported hair improvement (IRB-approved), 37.3% shedding reduction at 12 weeks, 92.6% less brittleness, 100+ active ingredients. Product language: "Hair Growth Kit," "personalized treatment plan," "AI scalp scan + quiz." Approved comparisons: Finasteride (side effects), hair transplants (invasive), generic OTC (not personalized), Nutrafol (price).

**Brand Compliance:** Extra vigilance on fonts, colors, logos — they WILL catch deviations. UGC scripts must be approved BEFORE production. DCT naming: DCT_[number]_[version].
