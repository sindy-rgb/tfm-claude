## 3. Brand Voice Rules

### NEVER Rules (Non-Negotiable)
1. **NEVER position Minoxidil as negative.** MDhair offers Minoxidil to some customers. Can position MDhair as better than **Finasteride** and **hair transplants**, but NOT Minoxidil.
   - Source: Pooja in Slack (Mar 4, 2026): *"For the Which hairline are you and the Comparison green screen concepts, we don't want to position Minoxidil as anything negative, as we do offer it to some customers. We can however, position ourselves as better than Finasteride and Hair transplants"*
2. **NEVER misspell the brand name.** It is "MDhair" -- no space between MD and hair, capital M and D. Not "MD Hair", "MdHair", "MDHair" (though internal docs sometimes use MDHair).
   - Source: Pooja flagged at DCT 129 review: *"brand name should be spelled as MDhair - it's incorrect at 00:04"*
3. **NEVER use a CTA other than "Take the quiz."** Do not rewrite to alternatives.
4. **NEVER omit the "up to 60% off" offer** in relevant creative.
5. **NEVER skip the AI-generated content disclaimer.** All AI-generated content must be labeled with an "AI-generated" disclosure.
6. **NEVER reference money-back guarantees without T&Cs.** Any guarantee claims need a terms and conditions reference indicator.
7. **NEVER use generic branded statics.** The client produces 10-15 of these internally per month and they haven't scaled. Don't recreate formats they've already exhausted.
8. **NEVER use fake or unverifiable claims.** No fake tweets, fabricated "featured in" claims, or anything that can be researched and proven untrue.

### Approved Language & Tone
- **Scientific but accessible** -- dermatologist-formulated, clinically proven, personalized
- **Emotional validation** -- especially for shedding, postpartum, confidence recovery
- **Key stats to reference:**
  - 88.9% of participants reported overall hair improvement (IRB-approved clinical study)
  - 37.3% reduction in shedding at 12 weeks
  - 92.6% noticed less hair brittleness
  - 100+ active ingredients (medicated and natural)
  - Dermatologist-formulated, AI-personalized
- **Product language:** "Hair Growth Kit" (hero product), "personalized treatment plan", "AI scalp scan + quiz"
- **Ingredient language:** sulfate-free, paraben-free, phthalate-free, vegan, color-safe
- **Approved comparisons:** Position against Finasteride (side effects), hair transplants (invasive/expensive), generic OTC products (not personalized), Nutrafol (price comparison)

### Brand Compliance
- **Fonts, colors, logos:** Extra vigilance required -- they WILL catch deviations
- **Naming convention:** DCT number + version (e.g., DCT_127_V1)
- **UGC scripts:** Must be sent for approval BEFORE pushing to production

## 5. Negative Triggers

### Creative Kills (Patterns That Have Failed or Been Rejected)
1. **Generic branded statics** -- Client produces 10-15/month internally, they don't scale. Don't waste concepts on this format.
2. **Overly AI-looking transformation content** -- Morph ad spend was reduced after users identified AI in comments. Test with more authentic/real-user imagery.
3. **Recycled angles the client has already exhausted** -- Always check what they've tested internally before proposing.
4. **Concepts without fresh differentiation** -- Client gets frustrated when work feels repetitive or "safe."
5. **Anti-Minoxidil positioning** -- Explicitly killed by Pooja. MDhair sells Minoxidil to some customers.

### Execution Red Flags the Client Has Called Out
- **Brand name misspelling** -- Pooja flagged "MDHair" vs "MDhair" and any spacing errors
- **Typos in video overlays** -- Pooja caught "snitch" instead of "stitch," "our eye" instead of "AI," "setting" instead of "shedding" in DCT 129
- **Blank space at end of videos** -- Flagged as sloppy
- **Editing inconsistencies** -- Called out at DCT 129 00:31
- **Missing disclaimers** -- AI content without disclosure, guarantee claims without T&Cs

### Client Sensitivities
- **Oded (CEO) flags brand compliance and disclaimer issues when he sees them** -- even though he's hands-off day-to-day, deviations surface up
- **Stock constraints** -- Has historically caused testing pauses (Aug 2025). Be aware of inventory limitations.
- **Oded's personal availability** -- Has been limited at times due to personal matters, which affects testing timelines since he pushes new ads live
