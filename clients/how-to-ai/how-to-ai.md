## 3. BRAND VOICE RULES

### NEVER Rules
1. **NEVER change the name "How to AI"**
2. **NEVER position as a "tool list" or "AI news aggregator"** — differentiated from The Neuron, TLDR, etc.
3. **NEVER use "replacement fear" hooks** (e.g., "AI will take your job")
4. **NEVER use tool-focused benefit frames** — teaches *how to think with AI*, not *what tools exist*
5. **NEVER use corporate/jargon language** — "Corporate language in = corporate language out"
6. **NEVER call it a "course" or "program"** — it is a free newsletter

### Approved Language
- **Core tagline:** "Master 'How to AI' with words, not code."
- **Core differentiation:** "They learn what to type, not how to think."
- **Voice:** Direct, nerdy, practical, anti-hype. Think "smart friend giving advice."
- **Key phrases:** "Stupidly practical AI guides," "Work faster & write better without becoming a prompt engineer"
