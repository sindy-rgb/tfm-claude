## Key Patterns

### What "Gold Standard" Looks Like (Lays — WorkWeek, JWS)
- Budget pacing % (not just spend)
- Dual-metric tracking (Sub CAC + Verified CAC)
- Per-newsletter breakdown (5 accounts for WW)
- beehiiv subscriber quality data (engaged %, open rate, verified CTR, unsub rate)
- Winning angle analysis (not just which ad, but WHY)
- Clear next steps / action items

### Common Gaps
1. **Most GMs send Thursday, not Friday** — despite the name "Friday report"
2. **Internal review before sending is inconsistent** — only Creator Spotlight and Insight Links have documented processes
3. **Client engagement correlates with report quality** — clients who get detailed reports respond more
4. **TPG doesn't follow Friday cadence** — uses bi-weekly sync calls instead
5. **Status News is dormant** — moved to email communication in Oct 2025
6. **n8n generates raw KPI drafts at 5-7 AM Fridays** — GMs are expected to polish with insights

### Clients With No Report Activity Found
- Student Loan Planner (no Slack channel)
- Open Source CEO (no activity in 60 days)
- Status News (dormant since Oct 2025)

---

## Recommendations

1. **Standardize "Thursday report" instead of "Friday report"** — that's when they actually go out. Align the name with reality.
2. **Template Lays' WorkWeek format as the standard** — it's what clients respond to best. Share as a training reference.
3. **Require internal draft review for at-risk clients** — Status News, Quartz, SLP, and any client with risk level MEDIUM or higher.
4. **Track on-time rate in weekly ops meeting** — Sindy already flags this; make it a visible metric.
5. **Add beehiiv/ESP quality data to all newsletter client reports** — JWS format proves clients value seeing engagement metrics alongside Meta performance.
