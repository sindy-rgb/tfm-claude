## Report Type Definitions

TFM runs two distinct client-facing report types. The original scorecard conflated them. Here is the corrected breakdown:

### Friday Report (Weekly Ad Performance Snapshot)
- **Target:** Every Friday by 12:00 PM EST (noon)
- **Channel:** External #thefeed-[client] Slack channels
- **Content:** Weekly spend, CPL, top/bottom performers, WoW changes, next steps
- **Process:** n8n bot generates raw KPI drafts at 5-7 AM Friday; GMs polish with insights and send by noon
- **On-time:** Sent Friday by noon. Late = sent after noon Friday, or sent on Thursday/Saturday/Sunday.
- **Note on Thursday sends:** The majority of "Thursday sends" in the source data are the Friday Report sent a day early. The content is identical to Friday sends (weekly performance snapshot, spend, CPL, WoW, top/bottom performers). These are NOT bi-weekly reports. However, sending on Thursday means the report covers only 6 days of data (Fri-Wed) instead of the full 7-day window (Fri-Thu EOD per the Sindy SOP in #internal-insightlinks). Thursday sends are classified as **early** -- better than late, but not aligned with the SOP.

### Bi-Weekly Report (Client Call Prep / Strategic Review)
- **Tied to:** Bi-weekly client sync calls
- **Content:** Broader strategic view -- creative pipeline, quality deep-dives, action items from calls, expectation setting, ROAS analysis, down-funnel metrics
- **Channel:** Often prepared in Notion; sometimes drafted in #internal-[client] channels; meeting recaps posted to external channels
- **Format:** Varies by client -- Notion docs, Loom recordings, Day.AI meeting recaps, Slack message summaries
- **Examples found in source data:**
  - TPG: Day.AI meeting recaps (Mar 10, Mar 20) -- no Friday report at all, only bi-weekly recaps
  - Franklin's Forum: Sindy reviewed bi-weekly draft (Mar 19) and flagged narrative gaps -- separate from the Friday report sent Mar 20
  - Vendry: Monthly Strategic Review and Mid-Month Pulse Check Notion templates (Feb 25)
  - Creator Spotlight: Jan 26 meeting recap from bi-weekly sync (explicitly marked "not a Friday report")
  - RNT Fitness: Kavit prefers Loom bi-weeklies over live calls due to timezone (Bali/Sri Lanka)
  - MDhair: Monthly call agreed; separate from any weekly reporting cadence

## Recommendations

### Friday Report Timing
1. **Enforce the noon Friday target** -- Only 12% of reports hit it. The two clearest patterns: Mariely sends Friday 2-4 PM (consistently 2-3 hrs late), and Lays/Luiz/Aubree/Humza send Thursday (a full day early, missing the final day of data). These are different problems requiring different fixes.
2. **For Mariely:** She already sends on Friday. The fix is simply moving her workflow earlier -- start polishing the n8n draft by 9 AM instead of noon. She manages the most clients (6) so this may require prioritization help.
3. **For Lays, Luiz, Aubree:** They are systematically sending on Thursday. This means they are reporting on 6 days of data (Fri-Wed) instead of 7 (Fri-Thu EOD per the Sindy SOP). Either (a) enforce Thursday EOD data cutoff + Friday morning send, or (b) officially shift the data window to Fri-Wed and accept Thursday delivery. Option (a) is better for data completeness.
4. **Rabii is the model** -- sends Friday morning 9-10 AM, well before noon, with complete weekly data. His MarketBeat workflow should be studied and replicated.

### Report Quality
5. **Template Lays' Workweek format as the gold standard for report depth** -- budget pacing, dual-metric tracking, winning angle analysis. Share as training reference for all GMs.
6. **Template Mariely's structure as the gold standard for consistency** -- "Happy Friday!" greeting, Google Sheet link, TL;DR, KPI table, insights, roadblocks, next steps. Every report follows the same skeleton.
7. **Add ESP quality data (open rates, engagement %) to all newsletter client reports** -- Lays (JWS), Mariely (1636, Franklin's), and Kinte (RNT) already do this. Others should adopt.

### Bi-Weekly Reports
8. **Standardize a bi-weekly report format** -- currently varies wildly (Notion, Loom, Day.AI, Slack messages). Consider a single Notion template that all GMs use.
9. **Require Sindy review on bi-weekly reports for at-risk clients** -- already happening for Franklin's Forum and Vendry. Expand to any client at MEDIUM+ risk.

### Process
10. **Track on-time rate weekly** -- add to ops meeting dashboard. Use the noon Friday target as the standard.
11. **Clarify the Thursday vs Friday question in the SOP** -- the current ambiguity lets GMs self-select their send day. Pick one and enforce it.
